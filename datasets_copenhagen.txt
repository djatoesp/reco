** Below are listed the continental / global links to the datasets required to run the R-ECO script:

* Urban Atlas / Street Tree Layer / Building Height: 
https://land.copernicus.eu/local/urban-atlas

* Digital Elevation Model: 
https://www.usgs.gov/centers/eros/science/usgs-eros-archive-digital-elevation-shuttle-radar-topography-mission-srtm-1?qt-science_center_objects=0#qt-science_center_objects

* Global Hydrologic Soil Groups:
https://daac.ornl.gov/cgi-bin/dsviewer.pl?ds_id=1566

* Global Biodiversity Information Facility:
https://www.gbif.org/occurrence/search

* European Protected Sites:
https://www.eea.europa.eu/data-and-maps/explore-interactive-maps/european-protected-areas-1

** These datasets must be referred to the boundaries of the case study area, which in the case of Copenhagen are available at the following link:

* Copenhagen districts: 
https://www.opendata.dk/city-of-copenhagen/bydele
